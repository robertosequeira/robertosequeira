# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Technical peers, potential collaborators, recruiters, and professional contacts who look up Roberto before a conversation, interview, or engagement. They arrive knowing what a senior engineering leader looks like; the site's job is to communicate *this specific person* — not to sell a service or convert a lead. Context of use: desktop or mobile, unhurried browse, likely direct navigation from a LinkedIn profile or a GitHub link.

## Product Purpose

Personal home base and professional credibility signal for Roberto Sequeira — Associate Director of Engineering, Costa Rica, 16 years building software. The site represents who Roberto is: honest work history, current focus (AI agents in practice), and personal commitments (Coopeldos). Not a consulting funnel, not a résumé facsimile. Success looks like: a visitor finishing the page with a clear, accurate sense of Roberto as a person and professional.

## Brand Personality

Honest, deliberate, warm-but-precise. Edited rather than polished; unhurried. The writing earns trust through specificity and modesty, not through credentials. The design should reinforce that register, not undercut it. Three words: *honest, composed, human*.

## Anti-references

- The old site: dark-blue gradient hero with floating skill chips — "strong writing, invisible design." No deliberate visual choice was made.
- LinkedIn-profile aesthetics: bullet hierarchies, keyword-dense, performatively professional.
- Generic AI-generated portfolios: cream/paper bg, display serif + italic kicker + mono labels + ruled separators. This lane is now saturated; see CLAUDE.md notes on anti-patterns removed.
- Overclaiming tech-bro personal brands: large numbers, metrics, "I scaled X to Y" social proof.

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint as voice, not absence of choice.** Every element is there on purpose. Nothing is decoration.
2. **Writing leads; design supports.** The copy earns trust through modesty and honesty. Design amplifies it, never contradicts it.
3. **Warm precision.** Human warmth (Costa Rica, family coffee, "some things you just don't let go") alongside engineering precision. Neither softens the other.
4. **One accent, used sparingly.** Terracotta (#B0471E) appears only where emphasis is earned. No gradient, no palette drift.
5. **No overclaiming.** The site says what's true at a high altitude. No employer specifics, no architecture details, nothing that would need defending.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum throughout (≥4.5:1 body text, ≥3:1 large display). `prefers-reduced-motion` disables all animation. `prefers-color-scheme` dark mode supported with warm dark palette. Bilingual: Spanish (`/`) is the default, English (`/en/`) mirrors structure exactly. Semantic landmarks, `aria-label` on sections, `hreflang` on language toggle.
