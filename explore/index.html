Blue — color psychology

Blue is one of the most versatile professional colors. The shade changes everything:

  • Dark navy — Authority, depth, expertise. Used by IBM, GitHub, Stripe. Signals seniority.
  • Bold/electric blue — Precision, innovation, digital-native. Technical confidence.
  • Soft/tonal blue — Calm, thoughtful, approachable. Intelligent but not cold.
